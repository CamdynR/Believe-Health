The Problem

Healthcare today is built around in-office appointments that are dictated by insurance companies and it isn't working...

  • Doctors jam more appointments into the day to maintain their income.
  • Insurance companies keep cutting payments to doctors offices and raising deductibles.
  • And often an office appointment is not needed to answer a question or to provide a diagnosis that could be covered through a virtual consult.
